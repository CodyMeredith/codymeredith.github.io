Purpose

The purpose of this page is to provide an easy-to-find forecast for the Boise metropolitan area. Especially because there are so many outdoor activities nearby, this site will make it easy for the avid outdoorsman or weekend warrior to properly plan for their outdoor adventure. There will also be information about local events, politics, and other stories relevant to the Boise metropolitan area to keep visitors informed.
